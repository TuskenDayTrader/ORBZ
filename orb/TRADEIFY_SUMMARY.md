## Confirmed / Verified excerpts (from Tradeify Help Center)

- Daily Loss Limit (DLL): "Daily Loss Limit (DLL): Resets each trading day. Hitting it pauses trading until next session. Soft breach—account stays active."  
  Source: https://help.tradeify.co/en/articles/10468321-rules-daily-loss-limit ✅

- Max Trailing Drawdown (general rule): "Your drawdown limit trails your highest-ever end-of-day balance (the 'high water mark') — it only moves up, never down... Hitting this limit FAILS your account permanently."  
  Source: https://help.tradeify.co/en/articles/10495897-rules-trailing-max-drawdowns ✅

- Consistency Rule (Growth): "No single trading day's profit should exceed 35% of your total profits."  
  Source: https://help.tradeify.co/en/articles/10468320-rules-consistency-rule ✅

- Microscalping rule: "Over 50% of your trades are longer than 10 seconds AND over 50% of your profit must come from trades held longer than 10 seconds. If you do not meet the criteria, you will not be able to activate the evaluation or request a payout."  
  Source: https://help.tradeify.co/en/articles/10495874-rules-news-trading ✅

- Hedging & mixing: "Tradeify prohibits hedging (opposing positions on the same instrument) and mixing MINI and MICRO contracts. These rules apply across ALL your accounts."  
  Source: https://help.tradeify.co/en/articles/10495868-rules-hedging-trading-micros-or-minis ✅

- Trading day / EOD timing: "A Tradeify trading day follows CME futures market hours: Market opens: 6:00 PM Eastern Time ... Market closes: 5:00 PM Eastern Time... All dashboard statistics update once daily during the 5-8 PM ET reconciliation window."  
  Source: https://help.tradeify.co/en/articles/10495876-rules-permitted-times-to-trade ✅
