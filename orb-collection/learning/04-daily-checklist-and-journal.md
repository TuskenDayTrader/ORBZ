# 04) Daily Checklist, Journal, and Paper Trading

## Daily ORB checklist

Use this checklist before and during trading.

### Pre-market

- [ ] I know which ORB timeframe I am trading today (5m, 15m, 30m, or 60m).
- [ ] I marked key levels from prior session.
- [ ] I checked today's major news/events.
- [ ] I set max daily loss and max trades.
- [ ] I committed to no rule-breaking.

### At setup

- [ ] Opening range is clearly marked.
- [ ] Breakout close is confirmed.
- [ ] Volume is acceptable.
- [ ] Stop and target are defined before entry.
- [ ] Position size follows risk limit.

### Post-trade

- [ ] I recorded entry, stop, target, and result.
- [ ] I recorded whether I followed rules.
- [ ] I wrote one improvement for next trade/day.

## Paper trading onboarding (simple path)

Week 1:

1. Trade only one timeframe (15m recommended).
2. Take screenshots of every setup.
3. Focus only on rule-following, not profits.

Week 2:

1. Keep same timeframe.
2. Track R results and rule accuracy.
3. Review false breakouts and missed trades.

Week 3+:

1. Add 30m or 5m only if rule-following is consistent.
2. Keep daily checklist and journal mandatory.
3. Move to live trading only after stable paper consistency.
